## Origin

A pictograph showing rain falling from the clouds in the sky. The top horizontal line represents the sky, the frame below suggests the clouds, and the four dots are raindrops falling to earth.

## Story

Look up at the sky and see the clouds heavy with moisture! The horizontal line is the vast sky, and inside the frame, you can see four distinct drops of water falling. Whenever you see this character, imagine the refreshing sound of rain on a roof.

## Position

Standalone kanji or becomes the TOP radical (amekanmuri)

## Transforms Meaning

Indicates weather phenomena, precipitation, or atmospheric states

## Example Kanji

### 雪 (snow)

Rain + a broom (⺕) = the white rain that you can sweep away like dust.

### 雲 (cloud)

Rain + rising steam (云) = the swirling moisture in the sky that eventually becomes rain.

### 電 (electricity)

Rain + a field with a tail (lightning) = the flash of light that strikes the ground during a rainstorm.
