## Origin

A pictograph of a flexed arm showing a bulging muscle, or an ancient plow being pushed through heavy soil—both representing the application of human strength.

## Story

Flex your arm and feel the power! That's 力, representing the raw muscle and effort required to change the world. Whether it's the strength of a warrior or the persistence of a farmer, this character is the mark of human agency and force.

## Position

Appears as a standalone kanji or on the right side of compounds

## Transforms Meaning

Indicates physical strength, mental effort, ability, or the application of force and power to a task

## Example Kanji

### 動 (move)

Heavy (重) + power radical = the act of applying strength to something heavy to make it shift or change position.

### 働 (work / labor)

Person (亻) + move (動) = a human being applying their power to create movement or results; the very essence of working.

### 助 (help / assist)

Power radical + another/further (且) = adding your own strength to someone else's effort to help them carry the load.
