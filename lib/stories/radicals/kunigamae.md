## Origin

A simple, complete enclosure representing walls, national boundaries, or any area that is fully contained.

## Story

Imagine a sturdy wall encircling a city! This radical completely wraps around other elements, containing them safely within its borders like a fence or a country's frontier. It represents the concept of being bounded, limited, or gathered within a specific space.

## Position

Surrounds other elements completely on all four sides

## Transforms Meaning

Indicates enclosure, containment, bounded areas, countries, or things that are gathered and kept within

## Example Kanji

### 国 (country / nation)

Enclosure (囗) + gem/altar (玉 simplified) = a precious land with its own borders and a central, protected treasure.

### 園 (garden / park)

Enclosure (囗) + a person planting (袁) = a sheltered, walled space where life is nurtured and cultivated by human hands.

### 図 (diagram / map)

Enclosure (囗) + elements/dots = information and shapes gathered and contained within a frame for study and direction.
